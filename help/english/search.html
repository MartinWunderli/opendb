Advanced Search

  • Boolean support is not available for 'exact' matches
  • Matches will be case INsensitive in all cases.
  • Use Boolean operators and nesting to search for combinations of words or phrases.
  • For Example:
    • Rambo AND III
    • II OR III OR IV
    • Rambo AND (II OR III)
    • ((2 OR II) AND (Rambo OR "First Blood")) NOT (III OR Box)
  • Use '_' wildcard for matching only one character and '%' wildcard for matching any number of characters.
  • For Example:
    • Searching for "se_en" could match, "seven" or "seben" or "sewen"
    • Searching for "%ve%" could match, "seven" or "fugitive" or "maverick" or "driver"
    • Searching for "ve%" could match, "version" or "vex" or "vehicle" or "driver"
  • If you use 'word' match, some titles may not show up if the item has a ':' (colon) as part of the title. Include the ':', or a wildcard to match such titles.
  • For Example:
    • The title 'Rambo: First Blood Part II', would not be matched in word mode by the query: Rambo AND II. You would need to specify %Rambo% AND II instead.
  • If you use wildcards with a "Title" or "Attribute" word, the Word / Partial match options will be ignored for that word.
