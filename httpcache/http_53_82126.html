Storyline

Prometheus, punished by Zeus, is chained to a mountain of Caucasus by god Hephaestus. There he starts to mourn for his tortures and narrate his story. Later, Io, with whom Zeus is in love, visits him, and he predicts the birth of a hero who will dethrone the king of the gods. Written by capamaroux@yahoo.gr
